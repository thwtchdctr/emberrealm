Privacy Policy

Ember realm does not collect or store any user information. All current online multiplayer games (VooDoo's Village and Lichfall) are peer to peer connections. All matchmaking is handled on your own machine and the session host's machine. All account services and online services are handled by Epic Games and their online Subsystem. If you have any questions, you can always reach out to us on the official Ember Realm website.

Ember realm may update this privacy policy to reflect the changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The last update to the privacy policy was made on 1/3/25
