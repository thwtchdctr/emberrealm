Better Left Forgotten

Better Left Forgotten is an Action-Adventure RPG set in a Lovecraftian Eldritch Old West that fuses deliberate high-stakes combat with a procedural world generation system where every action you take affects the world around you. Online multiplayer up to 3 total players.



Ember Realm's largest project to date.
